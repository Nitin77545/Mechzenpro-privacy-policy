Privacy Policy

MechZenPro Privacy Policy

MechZenPro respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and mobile number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Location details for service booking
  • Profile details such as skills and experience
  • Device and app usage data

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect customers with mechanics
  • Improve app performance and services
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Ensure safety and prevent fraud

3. Location Information

We use location data to show nearby mechanics and provide accurate service availability. Location access is used only while the app is in use and is never shared without permission.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Your information may be shared only:

  • With service providers to complete bookings
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect MechZenPro and its users

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your data
  • Request account deletion

7. Children’s Privacy

MechZenPro does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
